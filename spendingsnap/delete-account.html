Account Removal Consequences

Initiating account deletion triggers permanent destruction actions across our cloud storage layers. This operational cycle is **irreversible**:

  • Your secure user registration authentication login metadata will be deleted.
  • All historic parsed transactions logs and tracking snapshots are completely wiped.
  • Personal balance categories and configuration settings are erased.

Retained parameters: Exception log dumps and system crashes are kept as non-identifiable strings only when mandated by runtime tracking rules or security compliance laws.

Step-by-Step Native Application Deletion

The standard way to close your profile instantly is through the Android device layout interface:

  • Open the **SpendingSnap** app on your phone.
  • Tap your personal icon to open the **Profile** view dashboard.
  • Open the **Settings** sub-panel frame section.
  • Select the **Delete Account** option.
  • Complete the system confirmation alert challenge to finish the removal process.
