Delete Your Spending Snap Account

You can permanently delete your Spending Snap account directly from within the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Spending Snap.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Personal Information.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmation message.
  6. Confirm account deletion.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

The following information will be permanently removed:
  • Your Spending Snap account
  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials
  • Cloud profile information
  • Application preferences stored online

Local Data

All transaction history stored on your device is removed during account deletion. If you uninstall the application without exporting your data, your local transaction history cannot be recovered.

Retention

After account deletion, no personal account information is retained except where required by applicable law.
