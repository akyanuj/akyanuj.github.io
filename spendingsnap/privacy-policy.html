Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Spending Snap ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email Address

This information is used only to authenticate your account and enable synchronization features.

Transaction Data

Spending Snap stores financial transactions that you manually enter or import. This includes:

  • Transaction amount
  • Merchant name
  • Category
  • Date and time
  • Notes you create

Transaction information remains under your control and is never sold to third parties.

Notification Access

If you enable Smart Transaction Detection, Spending Snap requests Notification Listener permission. This permission is used only to detect supported UPI payment notifications from apps such as:

  • Google Pay
  • PhonePe
  • Paytm

Notification content is processed only to identify transaction details and create spending suggestions. We do not read personal conversations or unrelated notifications.

Photos & Screenshots

If you choose to import payment screenshots, Spending Snap processes the selected image locally using OCR to extract transaction details. Images are processed only when you explicitly choose to share or import them.

2. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services help us understand app usage and diagnose crashes. They do not collect your financial transaction history.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide expense tracking
  • Generate spending insights
  • Detect UPI transactions
  • Improve app performance
  • Fix crashes and bugs

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your financial transaction history with advertisers.

Only anonymous analytics and crash reports may be transmitted to Firebase.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. Communication with backend services is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete individual transactions
  • Export your data as CSV
  • Delete your account
  • Request deletion of associated cloud data

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly from within the app by navigating to:

Profile → Personal Information → Delete Account

Deleting your account permanently removes your authentication information and associated cloud data. Local transaction data stored on your device is also removed. This action cannot be undone.

8. Children's Privacy

Spending Snap is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
